A Legacy of Natural Preservation

Spanning 100 acres of private pristine woodlands, Cloud's End is surrounded by thick Oak and Deodar forests, offering a wide variety of flora and fauna. It provides a panoramic view of the snow-clad Himalayas and the Yamuna River.

Diverse species of flora and fauna have taken shelter in this beautiful hillside. This place also offers activities like hill climbing and nature walks in the nearby forest. Situated within 304 kilometers of Delhi, about a 6-hour drive away, this private forest sanctuary sits at an altitude of over 7000 feet.

This historic estate represents one of the last remaining private forest sanctuaries in the Himalayan foothills, maintained with dedication to environmental conservation and natural heritage preservation.
